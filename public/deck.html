09 / 15 · OPERATIONAL THINKING
Sella
How it stays innovative and scalable

Four operating principles that
keep both true at once.

01
Standardized core, local flavor
One playbook across sites — the morning ritual, the program structure, the platform, the brand. Menus, partners and themes adapt locally. Same chassis, regional skin.
02
AI does math, humans do hospitality
Sella forecasts attendance, predicts portions, drafts staffing. The team spends those hours on the floor — on plates, on people, on the parts a machine can't do.
03
Outcomes, not throughput
We stop measuring "covers." We measure NPS, attendance lift, recruitment mentions, waste reduction. Throughput is a means; outcomes are the contract.
04
Lean teams, deep skills
Fewer people per site, but each one operating closer to a hotelier or a brand director than a canteen supervisor. Empowered to spend, decide, and improvise.
Innovation is not a "lab." It's a default operating mode.

Execution · From idea to live program

Ninety days to a live, measurable rollout.

A pilot at TLV HQ, then a controlled expansion. No big-bang launches. Real numbers from day one.

Days 01 – 30 · Pilot
Tel Aviv HQ.
One site. One belief.
  • Install Sella. Establish baseline KPIs.
  • Soft-launch the morning ritual. Two breakfast experiments per week.
  • Open Wellness Tuesdays + Thursdays.
  • Train the on-site team on the new role profile.
  • Goal: prove the lift on NPS & attendance.
Days 31 – 60 · Refine + Replicate
Herzliya + London.
Test the playbook.
  • Roll Sella to Herzliya & London.
  • Tune for local preferences and time-zones.
  • First cross-site benchmarking dashboard live.
  • Hire / re-role two Experience Directors.
  • Goal: prove the playbook travels.
Days 61 – 90 · Global
Berlin + NYC.
Whole network live.
  • Berlin and NYC come online.
  • All five sites under cross-site KPIs.
  • First quarterly client business review with full dataset.
  • Lock the rollout template for the next 10 sites.
  • Goal: prove the model scales — and sells.
